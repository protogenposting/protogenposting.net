Strategies

It is recommended that all players start by playing matthew, as they are the character the whole game is balanced around.

Due to starting with a nearly full deck, Their original deck can be replaced very quickly. This can be useful since if played right you can have basically no normal notes by the late game

His starting health is also relatively high, so you can tank high difficulty songs early game and get high cost items quickly
