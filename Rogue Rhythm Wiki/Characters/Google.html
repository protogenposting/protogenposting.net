"THE GREEN"

Stats

Health: 20

Starting Deck

(16)

Starting Items

None

Active

On use, removes the note that is being pointed at by the pointer and throws it at the enemy (if there is one). Removing all notes in the deck instantly kills the player.

Passives

None

Strategies

> Google's ability has insane potential for longer runs. As long as you pick songs with uneven and dense notes, you should be able to fully control your deck.
> A funny strategy to pull off would be getting fire notes then tossing out all other note types, essentialy turning the song to autoplay.
> A much better strategy would be to only have healing notes to become unkillable while being able to still defeat enemies.
> Google overall is a very versatile character and will likely shred the entire game if used correctly.
