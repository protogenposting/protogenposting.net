About

Rogue Rhythm is a 4-key Rhythm game that shares elements with Deckbuilding Roguelikes. Survive as long as possible with your chosen Rogue from a selection of 8 characters and their given deck of notes, which greatly changes how each song plays during your run. How far can you get?

Rogues Gallery

As of release, Rogue Rhythms has a total of 9 Rogues to play with. Not only do they determine your starting deck, but your maximum health and some start off with additional items or unique passive abilities.
