About

Rogue Rhythm is a 4-key Rhythmn game that shares elements with Deckbuilding Roguelikes. Survive as long as possible with your chosen Rogue from a selection of 8 characters and their given deck of notes, which greatly changes how each song plays during your run. How far can you get?
