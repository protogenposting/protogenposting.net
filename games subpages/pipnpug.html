What is Pip N Pug Rollout?

PNPR is a physics-based 3D precision platformer where you play as pip, a small red bird, rolling around pug, an absolute ORB of a dog

the game is inspired by games from the marble blast "series" (and Patricia Taxon's video on the games) and aims to combine the best aspect of each game in the series
