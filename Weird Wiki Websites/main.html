What is this?

I am using this page to document weird wikis across the internet. This has been an interest of me for a while, since doing things like these wikis was something I did as a kid. I'd say this is definetly an autism thing.

The wikis are split into tiers from 1-20. 1 is a wiki that is just kinda weird and 20 is nearly incomprehinsible.

DM me on discord if you have any suggestions!

If you own any of these wikis, then feel free to tell me! I'd love to hear about it/get information on them!

Also I can not stress this enough:

DO NOT VANDALIZE THESE WIKIS. THEY DESERVER TO BE PRESERVED SINCE SOME OF THEM ARE PRETTY FUNNY/INTERESTING

The omnipotent UnAnything Team (consisting of the wiki's userbase) attempts to document everything that happens in the UnUniverse under the watchful eye of Captain 0: a digital, robotic guy who serves as the wiki's mascot (although every October that crazy Marasmus guy swaps him out with his arch-enemy Captain 1: the main antagonist of most UnAnything mythos).
