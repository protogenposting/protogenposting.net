What is Open Dance?

Have you ever played the game Just Dance and been like "Man I wish this game was made by a 17 year old and also had no pop music and also was open source"
Well you think oddly specific thoughts you need to go to therapy for that

Open Dance is essentially my version of Just Dance. However, it's gonna have a few differences to make it stand out

First of all, it'll be fully free and open source (mainly because i probably shouldn't monetize it or else ubisoft will come after me)

Secondly, a fully fleshed out custom song editor! The visuals for the song will just be video files while any extra data will be stored alongside it. You may be thinking "But i don't have any fancy motion tracking equipment!" Well i got you covered! All the movement for the song will be recorded through a mobile app! (android only sorry ios users) the app will also be used to play the game and select songs!

Lastly, no pop music! There's 2 reasons I'm doing this: 1. I don't like 99.999% of pop music and 2. I don't want the big record labels coming after me with a shotgun. Of course there's nothing stopping you from making your own pop music maps

Open Dance is an idea for a game I've had since i was at least 8 years old btw just thought i should mention that

If you know anything about godot (or gamemaker) game development or want to help with making maps for this game hit me up on discord! (my username is protogenposting)
